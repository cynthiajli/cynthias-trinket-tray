Miffy Coin Pouch

This is the Miffy Pochi Pouch Keychain, available for $16.00 at the MoMa Design Store. The pouch is made of a rubbery white silicone, with screen-printed black facial features. The clasp of the pouch is made from metal and also has a silver keyring attached to the back of one of the ears. The closure is a classic coin purse closure, but it was adapted to fit the shape of Miffy’s ears, which is very clever and allows the purse to have a clean design...

Sonny Angel "Cilla"

“Cilla” is the name of this Sonny Angel, a part of their collab with illustrator Donna Wilson. Released in a limited edition series, Cilla is a cartoon mouse, matching with many other animals depicted in the same style for this collection. The mouse costume differs from most other mouse Sonny Angels, with smaller and thicker ears and lines to represent the fur. Out of the whole Donna Wilson set, I have six Sonnys, and it is my biggest goal to collect every single one. This is a little hard though, as “Brian” (a stylized bunny rabbit with long ears) is one of the most sought-after Sonny Angels.

Rating: 5.0/5

Kirby Star Figurine

This is a small figurine of Kirby sitting atop a star. The figurine is flocked, so the entire thing is a little bit soft and fuzzy. It’s quite small, smaller than a lot of figurines I have, but also quite hefty in weight for its size. The box it came in wasn’t exactly a blind box: there’s a window where you can peek in and see what is inside, but there are around 10 options for what to pull. A lot of them are Kirby in various poses, but some are other characters from the game.
